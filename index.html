Welcome in my website

On this website I will be showcasing three of the best Japanese anime.

one-piece

Luffy is the hero of the One Piece series, a cheerful and brave young man who wears a straw hat and aspires to become the Pirate King.

Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates in the anime One Piece, aiming to become the greatest swordsman in the world.

Dragon-ball

Goku is the hero of the anime, a kind character who loves fighting and acquiring skills.

Jiron is the strongest fighter in Universe 11,mHe was one of the fiercest opponents in the Tournament of Power.

Vegeta is the arrogant and proud Saiyan prince who was once Goku's fiercest enemy but later became one of his closest friends.

Naruto

Sasuke is the sole survivor of the Uchiha clan and Naruto's main rival. He sought revenge against his brother Itachi and possesses superhuman visual abilities such as the Sharingan and Rinnegan.

Naruto is an orphaned ninja who carries within him the "Nine-Tailed Fox," who struggled as an outcast until he achieved his dream and became the "Hokage" and the world's hero.

Kakashi is the "Copy Ninja" and the leader of Team 7. He is famous for his sharp intelligence and Sharingan eye, and became the Sixth Hokage of Konoha Village.
